Minimum Requirements

Operating System:
Windows: Windows 8 or later (64-bit)
macOS: macOS 10.13 or later
Linux: Ubuntu 16.04 or later
Processor: Multi-core CPU with 64-bit support, such as Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 (or equivalent)
Memory (RAM): 8 GB or more
Graphics Card:

OpenGL 3.3 or higher compatible graphics card NVIDIA, AMD, or Intel graphics card with at least 1 GB VRAM recommended For GPU rendering, a CUDA or OpenCL compatible GPU is recommended Hard Drive Space: 10 GB of free hard drive space for installation and cache files


Recommended Requirements (for Better Performance):
Processor: Multi-core CPU with high clock speed (e.g., Intel Core i7 or AMD Ryzen 7)
Memory (RAM): 16 GB or more
Graphics Card:

A dedicated graphics card with 4 GB VRAM or more is recommended for complex scenes and GPU rendering. NVIDIA GeForce GTX series or AMD Radeon RX series graphics cards are good choices.
